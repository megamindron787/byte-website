About DevSphere
Where Ideas
Collide &
Friendships Form

DevSphere is a 5-day immersive technology festival for developers, designers, entrepreneurs, and curious minds. Think Google I/O meets a college carnival — energy cranked to maximum.

Keynotes from industry leaders, hands-on workshops, a 48-hour hackathon, gaming tournaments, TED-style talks, mentorship, and a different themed evening every night.

Whether you're a first-year student or a seasoned engineer, DevSphere challenges your thinking, expands your network, and fuels your next big idea.

Evening Theme: Grand Finale 🏆
Day 5 — Showcase & Finale
This must feel premium and structured. No chaos, no delays. Every moment is choreographed.

TED-Style Talks
